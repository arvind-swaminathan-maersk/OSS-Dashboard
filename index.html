📖 How to Use This Dashboard

This interactive coaching heatmap helps you identify improvement opportunities in OSS incident management across workstreams. Follow these steps:

🔄 Step-by-Step Workflow

1
Select your Workstream
Use the dropdown filter below the header. Choose "All Workstreams" for the full portfolio view, or pick a specific workstream to drill in.
2
Review the KPI Cards
The summary cards at the top update instantly — showing total cases, coaching flags raised, percentage flagged, and average response times.
3
Scan the Coaching Heatmap
Each cell shows a count and percentage. Darker red = higher proportion = bigger coaching opportunity. White/light cells are healthy.
4
Click any Heatmap Cell
A tooltip appears with specific DO / AVOID coaching advice for that flag and workstream.
5
Review the Coaching Flags Table
The "What They Mean" table shows each flag's definition, what to do, what to avoid, and the live count/percentage for your selected workstream.
6
Check Error Categories & Priority Mix
Understand the root cause distribution and whether priority levels are being used correctly via the donut chart.
7
Read the Coaching Summary
The bottom section auto-generates your top 2 priorities with specific, actionable advice.

💡 Tips

  • Use this dashboard in team retrospectives — project the heatmap and discuss the darkest cells together.
  • Track progress by comparing flag percentages month over month.
  • Share the direct URL with team leads — no Copilot licence is required.
  • Click column headers in the heatmap for a quick reminder of what each flag means.
